# ClearRead AI

ClearRead AI is a web-based AI literacy assistant that helps people in underrepresented and local communities understand complex documents by turning them into simple explanations, key points, and clear next steps.

Many people miss opportunities or face penalties simply because official language is difficult to understand. ClearRead AI removes that barrier.

## ğŸ§  How It Works

1. User pastes a complex document into the text box.
2. The backend sends the text to Google Gemini AI.
3. The AI analyzes the meaning and intent of the text.
4. Results are returned in three sections:
   - Simple Explanation  
   - Key Points  
   - What You Should Do Next
